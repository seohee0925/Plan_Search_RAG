### Document Anchor
Write a short stable natural-language anchor for downstream agents.
Prefer a compact noun phrase or short phrase, not a full-sentence summary.
Keep the anchor in the document's own surface language when possible.
Use a phrase that would still identify the document if only the title and a few key packets were visible.

### Region Roles
Write bullet lines in this exact format:
- [REGION_1] region_id=... | role=... | why=...
Rules:
- region_id must refer to one region from the Region Inventory.
- role should be a compact functional label such as metadata_region, lead_region, issue_region, reasoning_region, decision_region, table_region, attribution_region, comparative_region, or fallback_region.
- why should briefly say why this region may matter for later evidence search.
- If the document has multiple promising regions, include a likely starting region and at least one fallback region with a different role.
- Do not overuse metadata_region when later regions may contain stronger evidence.
- Emit only a small number of high-value regions.
- Do not use the pipe character inside field values.

### Divider Notes
Write 1-3 short sentences on how the document seems organized, where later agents should start, and when they should expand beyond the first region.
