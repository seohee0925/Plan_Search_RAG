### Evidence Units
Write bullet lines in this exact format:
- [UNIT_1] search_target=... | status=... | packet_refs=... | evidence_kind=... | extracted_text=...
Rules:
- status must be one of: grounded_candidate, scoped_no_hit.
- Extract evidence, not your conclusion alone.
- extracted_text should preserve the smallest sufficient raw span.
- extracted_text may be a sentence, a paragraph, or a short contiguous multi-paragraph span when one sentence is not enough.
- packet_refs should name one or more packet ids.
- Prefer exact or near-verbatim source text over summaries.
- Preserve metric labels, dates, units, and decisive judgment phrases when they matter for grounding.
- If multiple scoped packets are plausible, prefer the packet that states the decisive clue most explicitly.
- grounded_candidate means a real grounded span worth passing to the checker.
- Do not decide here whether the candidate span finally satisfies the task.
- For category or case-type targets, when the packets describe a concrete dispute, refusal, payment, benefit, qualification, entitlement, or administrative action, emit that wording as candidate evidence instead of suppressing it.
- If the packets contain a nearby formulation that may matter later, emit it as grounded_candidate instead of a scoped_no_hit.
- If the scoped view is insufficient, emit one concise scoped_no_hit unit instead of many negatives.
- Do not use the pipe character inside field values.
